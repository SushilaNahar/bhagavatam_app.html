Srimad Bhagavatam
Walking through the translations — one section at a time
The Ripened Fruit of the Wish-Fulfilling Tree of Vedic Literature
☰  Index

Spoken by Sukadeva Gosvami to Maharaja Pariksit on the bank of the Ganges, seven days before the King’s death. Presented section by section, with reflections for the sincere practitioner.

Progress
Canto 1 complete  ·  31 of 335 chapters
Canto One
Creation
19 chapters The departure of Krsna. The world learning to live in His absence. The arrow loosed in Chapter 1 lands in Chapter 19 on the bank of the Ganges.
Complete
Canto Two
The Cosmic Manifestation
10 chapters — CompleteSukadeva begins his answer. What should a man hear, chant, remember and worship? The cosmic form and the process of meditation.
Complete
Canto Three
The Status Quo
33 chaptersVidura’s pilgrimage. Conversations with Maitreya. The creation of the material world. The incarnation of Kapila and the teachings of Sankhya.
In Progress
Canto Four
The Creation of the Fourth Order
31 chaptersThe story of Dhruva. The story of Prithu. The Pracetas. The progressive development of human civilization and its relationship with the Lord.
Coming
Canto Five
The Creative Impetus
26 chaptersThe activities of Priyavrata and Rishabhadeva. The description of the universe. The hellish planets and the nature of material existence.
Coming
Canto Six
Prescribed Duties for Mankind
19 chaptersThe story of Ajamila — liberated by chanting the Lord’s name at the moment of death. The activities of Indra. The story of Citraketu.
Coming
Canto Seven
The Science of God
15 chaptersThe story of Prahlada Maharaja — the greatest devotee, the son of a demon, who knew Krsna from his time in the womb. The appearance of Lord Nrsimha.
Coming
Canto Eight
Withdrawal of the Cosmic Creations
24 chaptersThe churning of the ocean of milk. The appearance of the goddess of fortune. The story of Gajendra. The Vamana incarnation.
Coming
Canto Nine
Liberation
24 chaptersThe dynasties of the kings. The story of Ambarisa. The story of Rama and Sita. The lineage of Krsna and the preparations for His appearance.
Coming
Canto Ten
The Summum Bonum
The heart of the entire Bhagavatam
90 chaptersThe complete story of Lord Krsna — His birth, His childhood in Vrindavana, the Rasa dance, the killing of Kamsa, and His final departure. Larger than all other cantos combined.
Coming
Canto Eleven
General History
31 chaptersKrsna’s final instructions to Uddhava — the Uddhava Gita. The dissolution of the Yadu dynasty. Krsna’s return to the spiritual world.
Coming
Canto Twelve
The Age of Deterioration
13 chaptersThe prophecies of Kali Yuga. The glories of the Bhagavatam. Pariksit’s death and liberation. The closing of the circle begun in Canto 1.
Coming
